Dice Potions

Roll, brew, trade. Choose your path.
1) Roll
Roll your dice for gold and ingredients. Effects (gold boosts, shields, wilds) trigger automatically.
2) Brew
Add ingredients to the cauldron to match a recipe, then Brew. Potions can be consumed for effects or sold for gold.
3) Shop
Buy items, dice, and faces between rolls. Use reroll tokens or gold to refresh the shop. Permanent items add ongoing effects.
4) Next Round
After shopping, start the next round. Survive the set number of rounds with the most value you can brew and sell.
